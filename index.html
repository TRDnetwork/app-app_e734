Origin

Our beans are sourced from small farms in the Western Ghats, where rich soil and monsoon rains create the perfect conditions for Arabica beans with notes of dark chocolate and cherry.

Craft

Every batch is roasted in-house using traditional drum roasters, monitored by hand to bring out the subtle flavors that define exceptional coffee.

Location

Nestled in the heart of Karnal’s historic market district, our café will offer a warm, inviting space to enjoy slow mornings and great conversation.
